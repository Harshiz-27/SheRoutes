About SheRoutes

We believe every woman deserves to navigate the world confidently, safely, and freely.

💗 Our Mission

SheRoutes was born from a simple but powerful idea: navigation should feel safe, not just efficient. We combine real-time Google Maps data with community-powered safety insights to help women choose routes they can trust. Every feature on this platform — from safety scoring to one-tap SOS — was designed with one user in mind: you.
