🛡️ Safety Tips

Practical advice to navigate cities confidently and safely

🚨 Emergency Numbers India

📋 Before You Travel

📱
Share Your RouteSend your trip details and live location to a trusted contact before leaving.
🔋
Charge Your PhoneAlways travel with at least 60% battery. Carry a power bank for long trips.
🗺️
Know Your RouteReview your path on SheRoutes before leaving. Prefer well-lit, populated roads.
👗
Dress ComfortableWear shoes you can run in. Keep bags close to your body and avoid displaying valuables.

🚶 While Travelling

👁️
Stay AwareAvoid headphones on full volume. Be aware of your surroundings at all times.
💪
Walk ConfidentlyWalk with purpose, make eye contact, and project confidence. Predators target hesitant walkers.
🏪
Enter Public SpacesIf you feel followed, enter a busy store, restaurant, or bank immediately.
🚗
Verify CabsAlways verify the car number plate and driver photo before getting into a ride-share.
📞
Fake a CallIf uncomfortable, pretend to talk on the phone. Say "I'm just around the corner" loudly.
🌙
Avoid Dark AlleysAt night, stick to main roads with streetlights. Never use shortcuts through unlit areas.

📲 Digital Safety

🔒
Lock Your PhoneSet a PIN/fingerprint lock. Enable Find My Device in case of theft.
📍
Live Location SharingUse WhatsApp or Google Maps to share live location with a trusted person during the journey.
🚨
SOS on SheRoutesUse our one-tap SOS button to instantly alert emergency contacts with your exact GPS location.
